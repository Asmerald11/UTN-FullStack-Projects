My name is Camila Casolati, I am 22 years old and an artist. I introduced myself to the world of art at a short age when I decided to learn jazz dance. Then I studied drawing and painting ath the Grassi conservatory, I took a digital art course at the Da Vinci University in Buenos Aires, I have been part of art exhibitions and painting competitions both digital and painting. At the moment I am studying at the Instituto Berni of Buenos Aires the career of teacher of visual arts and I am working as a freelancer artist doing comissions.

I have knowledge in the management of social networks, edition and collation of photos and videos, I have coordinated charity events, I have experience in digital drawing and painting with acrilics and oils. Moreover I also have amateur knowledge in photography. I know how to use drawing and edition software, such as Adobe Photoshop CS6, Flash Professional, Sony Vegas Pro, Movie Maker, Corel Painter, Illustrator, amongst others.
